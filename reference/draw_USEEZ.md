# Bounds the figure with the limits of the U.S. EEZ

Bounds the figure with the limits of the U.S. EEZ

## Usage

``` r
draw_USEEZ(lon = c(-129.15, -116.5), lat = c(31.9, 49.5))
```

## Arguments

- lon, lat:

  Vectors of two real numbers specifying the longitudinal and
  latitudinal limits for the x and y axes.
